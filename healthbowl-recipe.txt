Ingredients:
 - 1/3 cup of quinoa
 - 1/3 cup of lentils
 - 1 tomato
 - 1 half avocado
 - 1 broccoli head
 - 1 sppon of olive oil
 - 1/2 tea spoon of Hymalayan salt
 - 1 half lemon

Directions:
 1. Cook the quinoa and lentil
 2. Steam the broccoli
 3. In a bowl, place the cooked quinoa & lentils, plus hald the avocado and the cut tomato.
 4. Add olive oil onto the broccoli, tomato and avocado.
 5. Add the lemon juice on avocado and tomato & add the salt.
